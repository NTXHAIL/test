Advanced Procedures

Corrosion Protection (R&I)

Always include 0.5 - 1.0 hours for corrosion protection if access requires drilling or if factory coatings are disturbed during the PDR process.

Scanning (Pre/Post)

Required for all vehicles 2016 and newer. Ensure line items reflect current OEM requirements for ADAS calibration if applicable.

Photo Checklist

  • VIN / Dash Odometer
  • 4 Full-Vehicle Corners
  • Dent Detail (With Grid/Light)
  • R&I Progress Photos

Stop & Review

If paint is cracked, the metal is sharply creased, or the panel is obstructed by reinforcements, switch to **Conventional Repair** or **Replacement** logic immediately.
